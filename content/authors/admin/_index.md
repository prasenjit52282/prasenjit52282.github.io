I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in the [Department of Economics](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/) at the University of Mannheim. I will be on the job market in 2026–27.

My research is at the intersection of **Financial Economics**, **Corporate Finance**, and **Industrial Organization**. I study how market structure and information shape financial contracting, litigation finance, and corporate governance.

I am advised by [Volker Nocke](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/nocke/) (Department of Economics), [Ernst Maug](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/maug/) (Department of Finance), and [Ernst-Ludwig von Thadden](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/von-thadden/) (Department of Economics).
